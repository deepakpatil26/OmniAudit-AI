Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

OmniAudit AI helps users audit product listings, packaging claims, and supporting compliance materials. This page explains what data is processed in the app and how it is used.

What We Collect

  • Account information provided through Firebase Authentication.
  • Audit inputs such as listing text, uploaded packaging images, marketplace screenshots, and optional supporting documents.
  • Generated audit outputs including findings, scores, rewrites, reminder settings, and product memory records.

How Data Is Used

  • To run AI-assisted compliance audits and explain results inside the workspace.
  • To save audit history and product memory in your Firebase-backed account.
  • To improve workflow continuity, such as re-audits, reminder cadence, and action tracking.

AI Processing

Audit prompts and related inputs may be sent to server-side AI providers to generate findings, rewrite suggestions, and compliance guidance. Do not upload sensitive information unless your deployment and provider configuration are approved for that use.

Data Control

You control the content you upload. Audit records stored in your configured Firebase project can be updated or deleted by authorized users according to your Firestore rules.
